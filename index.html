Certificate Eligibility Checker

CSI & MKITOS CTRL. ALT. ACT.

Use this tool to check if you're eligible for a certificate. There are two certificate categories:

  • Achievers Certificate: For participants who completed all assignments, quizzes, and submitted the OSINT Lab.
  • Completion Certificate: For those who completed at least 3 quizzes and 3 assignments and submitted the final quiz.

If eligible, you'll be redirected to the download portal. Please enter your name carefully there. Only one certificate will be issued per email.

⚠️ Note: If you are listed as Achievers, you will not be given a Completion Certificate. Only the highest certificate is awarded.
